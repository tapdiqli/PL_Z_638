Reviews from dedicated comparison sites tend to be more balanced. They walk through the things that actually matter — the game library, the payment options, the licence, the site layout and the overall feel — giving you a cleaner overview before you decide to join.

Checking if an Online Casino Is Safe

There's always an element of risk when you gamble, but that risk should come from the game itself — not from worrying about the site. Dodgy operators still exist online, so the safest approach is to stick with UK casinos that are properly licensed. Any casino operating legally in the UK is regulated by the UK Gambling Commission, and if you can't spot that licence, it's better to walk away.
